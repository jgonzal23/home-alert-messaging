The Home Alert Messaging Program, operated by Juan Gonzalez, sends transactional, informational SMS notifications to users who have opted in. This program does not send marketing or promotional messages.

Opt-in

Users opt in by voluntarily enabling SMS notifications and providing their mobile phone number as part of the opt-in process. By opting in, users agree to receive SMS messages from Juan Gonzalez (Home Alert Messaging Program). No messages are sent without user-initiated consent.

Proof of consent is documented on the Consent (Opt-In) page.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on user settings and event activity.

Opt-out

Reply STOP to opt out at any time.
