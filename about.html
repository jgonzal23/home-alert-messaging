About Home Alert Messaging

Home Alert Messaging is a residential notification service operated by Juan Gonzalez. The service provides transactional, event-driven SMS notifications to users who have explicitly opted in.

The purpose of the service is to deliver timely informational updates related to home monitoring and automation systems. These notifications help users stay informed about important events affecting their residence.

Examples of notifications include:

  • Security and entry status updates
  • Garage door and access notifications
  • Water leak and environmental alerts
  • System health and operational status

The service is designed for informational purposes only. No marketing or promotional messages are sent.

Users maintain full control over notification settings and may opt out at any time.
