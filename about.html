About Home Alert Messaging

Home Alert Messaging is a residential notification service operated by Juan Gonzalez. It provides transactional, event-driven SMS notifications to users who explicitly opt in.

The service delivers timely informational updates related to home monitoring and automation systems. Messages are informational only and never marketing or promotional.

Examples of notifications include:

  • Security and entry status updates
  • Garage door and access notifications
  • Water leak and environmental alerts
  • System health and operational status

Users control which notifications are enabled. Message frequency varies based on event activity. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out: Reply STOP to unsubscribe at any time.
Help: Reply HELP for assistance.
