<h1>Home Alert Messaging Program</h1>

 

<p class="muted">

This page describes a private, internal SMS messaging program used to deliver

<strong>transactional system alerts</strong> from a Home Assistant smart home installation

to a single authorized user (the system owner).

</p>

 

<div class="box">

  <h2>Program Details</h2>

 

  <div class="kv">

    <div class="k">Program name:</div><div>Home Alert Messaging</div>

    <div class="k">Message purpose:</div><div>Transactional system notifications only</div>

    <div class="k">Message types:</div>

    <div>

      Security events, garage door status, water leak alerts, environmental warnings,

      system health notifications

    </div>

    <div class="k">Audience:</div><div>Single authorized user (system owner)</div>

    <div class="k">Message frequency:</div><div>Event-driven; varies (typically fewer than 5 messages per day)</div>

  </div>

</div>

 

<div class="box">

  <h2>Opt-In & Consent</h2>

  <p>

    The recipient explicitly opts in by entering their own mobile phone number

    during system configuration and enabling SMS alerts.

  </p>

  <p>

    Enrollment is controlled by the user. No third-party numbers are added.

  </p>

</div>

 

<div class="box">

  <h2>Opt-Out & Help</h2>

  <p>

    Reply <strong>STOP</strong> to opt out at any time.

  </p>

  <p>

    Reply <strong>HELP</strong> for assistance.

  </p>

</div>

 

<div class="box">
