Home Alert Messaging Program

The Home Alert Messaging Program provides transactional, event-driven SMS notifications to users who have explicitly opted in. Messages are informational only and are not marketing or promotional in nature.

Program details

Program name: Home Alert Messaging

Use case category: Account Notifications

Message purpose: Transactional notifications requested by the user.

Typical message types:

  • Security and access-related status notifications
  • Garage/entry status notifications
  • Water leak and environmental notifications
  • System health notifications

Estimated frequency: Event-driven; varies based on user settings and events.

Note: Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-in (consent)

Users provide consent by voluntarily enabling SMS notifications and providing their mobile number as part of the opt-in process. No messages are sent without user-initiated opt-in.

Proof of consent is documented here: Consent (Opt-In).

Opt-out and help
